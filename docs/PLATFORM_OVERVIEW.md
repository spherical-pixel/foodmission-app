## FOODMISSION Platform Overview
### 👤 User
**User Profile:** A basic profile will be mandatory when registering. Adding later an extended onboarding with additional data as dietary preferences, income level or other additional optional data.

#### - Basic profile:
* Username
* Age
* Country
* Region
* ZIP code
* Language

#### - Extended profile:
* Gender
* Dietary preferences
* Annual income
* Level of education

**User preferences:** We will also store user preferences for the app, as well as avatar data. This will enable the user to opt in/out of some modules or data sharing.
* Nutri bars progress (Health, Sustainability, Knowledge)
* Avatar personalization
* Enabled modules
* Dashboard customization
* Data sharing preferences
* Text size
* Theme
* Background
* Font style
* Push notifications enabled
* Sound and music preferences

**Technical data:** Additional technical data might be collected in order to ensure platform functionality. 
* Device Token identifier (for push notifications)
* 

**Groups:** Groups of users to share e.g. shopping lists, recipes, or progress of missions. Every user will have the possibility to create or join a family group.
* Name
* Type
* Members

### 🛒 Shopping & Pantry
**Shopping List:** A list of items you or your group need to buy. Products in this list will be added to Pantry if checked as bought. Possibility of having list "templates" to speed up the process of share with other participants.
* Product
* Quantity

**Virtual Pantry:** A digital inventory of the food you have at home, helping you track quantities and reduce waste. Including expiry dates of your inventory.
* Product
* Quantity
* Expiry date

**Food Product:** Detailed information about an item, including its brand, nutritional facts, allergens, and eco-score.

### 🍽️ Meal Logging
**Food Diary:** A chronological history of your logged meals, including breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks. A meal can be analyzed on its value and ecological impact. A meal can be logged with all details adding every item or just add categories and aproximate quantities consumed to make it easier.

It will be logged if the meal is at home or outside and if needed remove items consumed from pantry.


**Quick Log:** A streamlined way to log food consumption using a barcode scanner or a quick search.

**Personal Statistics:** Visual charts and data summaries regarding your nutrition, carbon footprint, and platform activity over time.

### 📖 Recipes
**Recipes:** A collection of community-shared and personal recipes, featuring instructions, prep times, and difficulty levels. Users will be able to create, share and score them.

### 🏆 Challenges & Missions
**Daily Challenges:** Short-term tasks refreshed daily that reward you for completing specific healthy or eco-friendly actions.

**Missions:** Long-term journeys or quests that you can start and complete to earn rewards and track your progress.

Challenges & Missions will unlock content and award badges to the user.
 
### 🗑️ Food waste
**Food waste:** Track food waste, it will popup when it detects some product in the pantry it's expired, it will also allow individual waste input. It will provide information about Carbon footprint and economic cost of the wasted products.


### 💡 Knowledge
**Knowledge:** Specific categories of food-related learning, such as nutrition or sustainable sourcing. It will contain learning paths for different topics.

It's still not clear the format but we will collect data about the items viewed, progress, and interactions.

### 🎮 Games
**Games:** Small games to learn new things or just for fun. It will be linked with Knowledge and Challenges & Missions to make the learning more fun. It will collect data about attemps, score, and progress.

### 🌐 Global Community
**Global Community:** It will represent your progress and your groups progress in the global community, allowing different kinds of filters. It won't log any data.


### 📍 Map of sustainable businesses
**Map of sustainable businesses:** A map that shows sustainable businesses nearby.

It's still not clear yet, but user's will be able to send a request for adding new places.

Users can add places to favourites list to have them handy.
